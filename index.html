How to use
1. Set Foil / Non-Foil toggle before scanning Commons & Uncommons.
2. Tap Start Scanning — camera activates.
3. Slide each card into your stand, name facing the camera.
4. App captures frames and reads card text via OCR.
5. Confirm the card name before it's saved.
6. Rares & Legendaries always prompt for foil individually.
7. Tap Stop Scanning when done.
